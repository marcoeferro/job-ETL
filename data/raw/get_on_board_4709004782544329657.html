Birdie is transforming the currency of gifting – and building the future of social fintech along the way.

We believe in learning by doing. As a part of our team, you’ll have the opportunity to flex (and gain) different muscles by solving challenging engineering and fintech problems. To date, we have secured a banking partnership, built a digital wallet with P2P payments, developed best-in-class KYC/AML procedures, launched our beta – and we’re building a world-class team and culture.

Apply to this job directly at getonbrd.com.

Job functions

You’ll be joining our fully remote engineering team to work on our Django Rest Framework-based backend.

As a backend engineer, you can expect to work on all aspects of our platform. You’ll collaborate with our product/frontend team as they build user interfaces and work with other back-end engineers to design well-tested and performant APIs.

Responsibilities

  • Write clean, well-tested and modular code
  • Designing highly scalable and flexible backend systems
  • Develop RESTful APIs using the Django REST Framework
  • Integrate various libraries/frameworks and APIs
  • Write tests for all views, models, serializers

Deliverables

  • Django models, serializers, views
  • Integrations with APIs, libraries, and Python packages
  • Unit and Integration tests

Qualifications and requirements

The ideal candidate

  • Has 3+ years Django Rest Framework and Python
  • Has experience with Test Driven Design and writing unit tests
  • Ability to communicate clearly
  • Ability to set and meet deadlines on time
  • Minimum C1 Level English
  • Must be located in between UTC-9 and UTC-3 (North America, LATAM, or South America)

Nice to haves

  • Experience building payments platforms
  • Experience with React
