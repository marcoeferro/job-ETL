Series Entertainment is building the future of interactive entertainment with a fast-paced Go To Market team shaping growth for mobile games. This role sits within our mobile UA function, collaborating with product, data, and creative teams to design and execute scalable campaigns across multiple channels. You’ll contribute to a high-velocity environment that values experimentation, measurable impact, and rapid iteration, while ensuring alignment with product KPIs and player lifecycle objectives. The position offers remote flexibility within the South America region with a 3-month contract term and potential for extension based on performance.

This job is published by getonbrd.com.

Key Responsibilities

  • Set up and run art tests and test market campaigns to validate creative and messaging hypotheses.
  • Analyze campaign results and communicate insights to developers to inform product improvements.
  • Execute the global user acquisition post-launch strategy across paid social, SDKs, DSPs, search, influencer channels, and emerging platforms.
  • Optimize ad spend allocation to maximize ROAS across multiple games and campaigns.
  • Design, launch, and scale campaigns with rigorous A/B testing and creative iteration.
  • Collaborate with product, data, and creative teams to ensure campaigns align with in-game KPIs and player lifecycle goals.
  • Monitor industry trends, new tools, and platform updates to keep UA efforts cutting edge.

Role Overview

We are seeking a creative, data-driven Mobile User Acquisition Marketer who thrives in a fast-paced environment. You’ll blend sharp analytics with bold campaign execution to attract and retain high-value players. The ideal candidate has hands-on experience with mobile UA, strong communication skills, and a passion for gaming. You will work within a dynamic Go To Market team and contribute to scalable growth strategies across multiple titles, channels, and markets. Fluency in English is required for day-to-day collaboration.

Nice-to-Haves

AI and tech-savvy mindset; experience with influencer marketing or organic growth; familiarity with soft launches, global launches, and live service growth; brand marketing experience; cross-platform and web UA; collaboration with cross-functional game development teams; deep interest in gaming and understanding of player behaviors; ASO/SEO or other organic growth skills.

What We Offer

Join a dynamic team crafting compelling game marketing content in a remote-friendly environment. This contract role offers a balanced schedule, the opportunity to work with cutting-edge creative and AI tools, and a platform to showcase your ability to produce impactful visuals at scale. You’ll be part of a creative, fast-paced Go To Market team that values curiosity, experimentation, and rapid iteration. Remote-friendly with a 3-month commitment and potential for future opportunities based on performance.
