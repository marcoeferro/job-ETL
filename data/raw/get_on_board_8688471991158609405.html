In Interfell we’re looking for a Marketing Project Manager to act as the central link between cross-functional teams in Marketing, Product, and Design, ensuring flawless execution of campaigns and launches. The culture is built on accountability, transparency, and teamwork, with the constant challenge of enhancing customer experience and maintaining brand consistency across every touchpoint
In this project we’re focused on strengthening our brand positioning through integrated marketing and communication strategies. We operate in a highly collaborative, results-driven environment where innovation, creativity, and efficient project management are core values.
You should be fluent in English (written and spoken) and possess a strong understanding of marketing workflows, creative processes, and project management tools (e.g., Asana, Figma).

Remote 100%
English C1 required.
Salary : 1,500 - 2,000 (USD) monthly

Applications: getonbrd.com.

Responsibilities

  • Create and manage the marketing deliverables timeline from kickoff to delivery, ensuring alignment across all milestones.
  • Schedule and track key project checkpoints: product/design handoff, marketing briefs, copy deadlines, creative reviews, and web/PDP/PLP approvals.
  • Maintain and update the shared integrated marketing calendar with GTM deadlines and launch details.
  • Provide regular progress reports, ensuring transparency and proactively addressing blockers.
  • Manage product samples logistics—placing and tracking orders, monitoring inventory, and coordinating with Tech Design and Sourcing for photoshoots and influencer content.
  • Partner closely with Brand, Creative, Growth, Product, and Operations to align project needs and ensure cross-functional clarity.
  • Create, distribute, and manage trackers for visibility and accountability across teams.
  • Maintain rigorous organization of all project documents, assets, and files for easy access and long-term reference.

What we’re looking for

  • 3+ years of Project Management or Marketing Operations experience at a fast-growing ecommerce company
  • English language proficiency
  • You thrive on organization, structure, and details, and love building systems that make complex projects feel seamless.
  • You’re collaborative and approachable, with the ability to manage cross-functional relationships at all levels.
  • Unshakable ability to manage multiple projects without letting anything slip through the cracks.
  • Strong understanding of marketing workflows, creative processes, and project management tools like Asana and Figma
  • Performance mindset with ability to proactively present risks, opportunities and solutions
  • Thrives in fast-paced startup environments and comfortable rolling up sleeves
  • You have the mentality of work smarter, not harder

Desirable skills and experience

Proficiency in Asana and Figma; knowledge of eCommerce industry dynamics; proven ability to lead cross-functional teams; strong leadership, problem-solving, and decision-making capabilities; excellent written and verbal communication; creative thinking and attention to detail; adaptability to changing priorities and fast deadlines.
